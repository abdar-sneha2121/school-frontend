OUR STORY

Our Journey of Excellence

HOLYFAITH ENGLISH SCHOOL, RUKADI, established in 2007, is a trusted educational institution committed to providing quality education rooted in strong values and holistic development. Founded under the leadership of Mr. Madhukar Wayadande and Principal Dr. Nisha Tandale, the school focuses on nurturing academic excellence, discipline, and character building from an early age.

Guided by our motto, “We Shape Your Future”, we offer a supportive learning environment with modern facilities, innovative teaching methods, and dedicated educators. With over 300 students and 20+ experienced teachers, HOLYFAITH ENGLISH SCHOOL prepares students to become confident, responsible, and future-ready individuals.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution that empowers students with knowledge, skills, and values to become responsible citizens and successful leaders in a rapidly changing world.

Our Mission

To provide quality education through innovative teaching methods, nurturing environment, and holistic development programs that prepare students for academic excellence and life success.
